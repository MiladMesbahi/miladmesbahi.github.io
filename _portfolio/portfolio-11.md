I worked through the full design-to-fabrication pipeline for a mini chair component, using both manual machines and CNC to go from stock to finished parts. Even though I don’t do much machining now or really plan to in the future, it gave me solid hands-on experience with tools, fixturing, tolerances, and CAM/G‑code, and a good intuition for how designs actually get made in the shop.
