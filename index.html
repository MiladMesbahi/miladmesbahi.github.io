About Me

As an aspiring mechanical engineer with a passion for space exploration and technology, I am dedicated to pursuing innovative solutions that will empower humanity. My diverse technical skills in areas like robotics, programming, and research have equipped me to tackle complex engineering problems. However, I believe technological advancement must also focus on creating sustainable solutions that benefit society as a whole. I strive to use my education and abilities to develop technologies that will open up new possibilities in fields like renewable energy, space travel, autonomous systems, and more. My aim is to collaborate with talented teams to ideate, design, and engineer systems that will drive forward positive change on both global and local levels. Whether it's building rovers destined for other planets or apps that connect communities here on Earth, I am committed to engineering technology ethically and responsibly to create an uplifting future for all.
